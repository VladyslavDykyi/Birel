In 2010 the number of secondary transactions was 500+ per year in 2021 the number of transactions exceeded 2500.

In 2010 the volume of secondary transactions is 9 billion in 2021 the volume of transactions is 48+ billion.

Significant growth is due to the fact that more companies remain private for a longer period, the average path to an IPO is 12 years. 15 years ago, the average path to an IPO was 6 years.
